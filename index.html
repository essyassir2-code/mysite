📜 GLORIA RULES

  • 1. Respect — All players must be treated with respect. Zero toxicity.
  • 2. Roleplay first — Stay in character at all times. No Random Deathmatch.
  • 3. Value of Life — If a gun is pointed at you, you must cooperate.
  • 4. No Metagaming — Don't use out-of-character info in character.
  • 5. Realistic driving — No unrealistic stunts or cars in the air.
  • 6. New Life Rule — If you are downed and revived, you don't remember last 15min.
  • 7. Staff authority — Their word is final. Disputes after the session.
  • 8. Gangs & Turfs — Maximum 10 members per gang, war rules apply.
  • 9. Economy — No real money trading, only server donations (VIP).
  • 10. Have fun! — But always keep the RP quality high.
